You're able to correct or update much of your information on your personalized reservation page here. Whenever you wish, you may enter bid sheet details about your pieces there as well. Add a piece by clicking the "Add Piece" button and filling out its title, what gallery you will display it in, price (if the piece will be for sale), and artwork medium, then clicking "Save." Each piece you've saved will be shown in a list, along with the information you filled in. Edit a piece by clicking the blue pencil icon next to the piece details, or delete a piece by clicking the red "x" icon instead. You can do this up until you check in with us at the con, at which time we'll print the bid sheets and give them to you to attach to your artwork.
